Glass House offers weekly scheduled classes for dancers of all ages & ability levels, in the core dance genres of: Jazz, Ballet/Pointe, Hip Hop, Contemporary/Modern/Lyrical, and Acrobatics. With classes organized by genre, age & skill set, you dancer is sure to find their perfect fit! We are proud to offer all dancers two different optional performance opportunities, annually. For dancers who want even more stage time, we offer Performance and Competitive Programming! For our dancers 14 years and older who are interested in a career within the arts, we extend paid student teacher-internships.
